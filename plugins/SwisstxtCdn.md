SWISS TXT CDN
=============
Purges URLS from the SWISS TXT CDN.

Hooks
-----
- `cdn_purge`

Configuration
-------------
```yaml
plugins:
  swisstxt_cdn:
    username: foo
    password: bar
```

#### username
This is the username of the SWISS TXT CDN account you want to use

- Type: `String`

#### password
This is the password of the SWISS TXT CDN account you want to use

- Type: `String`
